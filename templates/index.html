Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Edusphere different from other educational programs?

Edusphere uniquely blends education, technology, and psychology to create experiential learning programs. Our curriculum is aligned with global standards while being tailored for the Indian context, and we focus on practical, hands-on learning that goes beyond traditional classroom teaching.

How can my school implement Edusphere programs?

Implementing Edusphere is simple and minimally disruptive to your existing schedule. We work closely with your administration to integrate our programs seamlessly. Our team provides all necessary training for your teachers and ongoing support throughout the implementation process.

What age groups do you serve?

We offer programs for students from kindergarten through 12th grade, with age-appropriate content for each level. Our curriculum is designed to grow with students, building foundational skills early and developing more advanced competencies as they progress.

How do you ensure program quality?

We maintain rigorous quality standards through continuous teacher training, regular program assessments, and feedback mechanisms with both students and educators. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest educational research and global best practices.

What is your exclusivity policy within a 5km radius?

To ensure our partner schools maintain a competitive advantage, we limit our partnerships to one school within a 5km radius. This policy helps maintain the uniqueness of our programs for your institution and prevents market saturation in your area.

How do you support students with special learning needs?

Our Empathy Integrated Education program includes specialized support for students with learning differences like dyslexia and dysgraphia. We provide tailored learning approaches, assistive technologies, and teacher training to create an inclusive learning environment for all students.
