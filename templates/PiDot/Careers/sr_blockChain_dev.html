Senior Blockchain Developer

Remote (Global) Full-time Engineering
Apply Now

About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Blockchain Developer to join our core engineering team working on PI DOT's Genesis network. You'll be responsible for designing and implementing core blockchain protocols, optimizing consensus mechanisms, and contributing to our open-source ecosystem.

This is a high-impact role where you'll work directly with our CTO and lead engineers to solve some of the most challenging problems in decentralized systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement core blockchain protocols for PI DOT's network
  • Optimize consensus algorithms for performance and security
  • Develop smart contract systems and virtual machine improvements
  • Collaborate with research team to implement cutting-edge cryptography
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical documentation
  • Participate in open-source community development and code reviews
  • Identify and resolve performance bottlenecks in our node software

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in software development with 2+ years focused on blockchain
  • Deep understanding of blockchain fundamentals (consensus, cryptography, P2P networks)
  • Proficiency in Rust (preferred) or Go/C++ with willingness to learn Rust
  • Experience with Substrate framework or other blockchain development frameworks
  • Strong knowledge of distributed systems and network protocols
  • Experience with cryptography concepts applied to blockchain (ZKPs, signatures, hashing)
  • Contributions to open-source blockchain projects (please include links in application)
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work independently

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Polkadot/Substrate ecosystem
  • Knowledge of WebAssembly (WASM) and its use in blockchain
  • Background in academic research related to distributed systems
  • Experience with formal verification of smart contracts
  • Contributions to blockchain standards or protocol specifications

How to Apply

Please send your resume/CV along with a cover letter explaining why you're interested in this position and how your experience aligns with our requirements. Include links to any relevant projects or GitHub repositories.

Our hiring process consists of:

  1. Initial screening call (30 minutes)
  2. Technical interview with engineering lead (60 minutes)
  3. Take-home coding challenge (3-5 hours)
  4. Final interview with team members (2 hours)
Apply via Email
